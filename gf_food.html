Gluten-Free Baking (and cooking!)

While I am not gluten intolerant, my partner is. Early on in dating, she mentioned missing croissants more than anything else, and I made it my mission to recreate them for our first Valentine’s Day together. I quickly realized I’d taken on one of the most technically demanding bakes — one that even professional chefs struggle with. So I studied the science behind gluten-free baking (shoutout to PhD chemist/baker the Loopy Whisk), embraced my inner scientist, and learned to troubleshoot, iterate, and perfect recipes. What started as a single challenge turned into a full-blown side passion!

My pride and joy. Gluten-free croissants which took a LOT of trial and error. My pro tip is to laminate your dough on a frozen cookie sheet to keep the butter from melting!.
